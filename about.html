About Savory Minds

Michael Chu

Professional Chef & Food Scientist

With over 15 years of experience in professional kitchens and food research laboratories, Michael brings a unique perspective to culinary arts. His mission is to bridge the gap between scientific understanding and practical cooking techniques.

After graduating from the Culinary Institute of America and completing a Master's in Food Science, Michael worked in Michelin-starred restaurants before transitioning to food product development and culinary education.
